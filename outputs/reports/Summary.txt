Canadian Banks Analysis — Summary

This analysis compares major Canadian banks, using both fundamental metrics (ROE, ROA, leverage) and market performance (1-year returns).

Findings:
- A weak positive relationship exists between ROE and stock returns
- Larger banks like RBC show strong profitability but moderate returns, suggesting stable market positioning
- Some smaller banks show stronger returns relative to fundamentals, indicating possible market-driven expectations

Conclusion:
Market pricing is influenced by more than profitability alone, including growth expectations and risk perception.
